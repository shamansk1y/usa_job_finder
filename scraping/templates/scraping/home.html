This is a non-commercial service for collecting and distributing vacancies, developed for demonstration purposes.

Every day we analyze sites with vacancies, according to the settings of our subscribers. All suitable vacancies that have been published on these sites over the past 24 hours are recorded in the database and sent to subscribers according to their preferences. You can browse current vacancies by simply selecting the required city or specialty. Or, after completing a simple registration, you will receive a letter every day with a list of new vacancies for the last 24 hours, according to your needs. The service is currently checking relevant vacancies on websites: indeed, linkedin, zip_recruiter, dice, ladders, flexjobs , builtin, themuse
